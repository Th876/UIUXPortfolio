The Rise of the UI/UX Unicorn

Hi! I'm Tahaylia Higgins, a Jamaican-American junior UI/UX designer with a background in Full-Stack Web Development and Foreign Languages. I am highly passionate about functional design because it cultivates a seamless and intuitive experience, building a bridge to loyal users!

While seeking employment, I yearned to quench my creative thirst and found solace in video editing through Adobe Premiere Pro, photo editing, and logo design with Adobe Photoshop.
